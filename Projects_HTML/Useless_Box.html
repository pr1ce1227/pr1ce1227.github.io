Useless Box

Main Concepts

  • 3D Printing
  • Servo Motors
  • State machine

Materials Used

  • Arduino Uno
  • 2 Stepper Motors
  • AA batteries
  • Custom 3d printed parts

Summary

You have probly seen one of these guys on the internet as I did many years ago. I decided to try and build one myself and I wanted to challenge myself to build it complelty from scratch with no tutuorials. I was able to do this by using a few Servo Motors and an arduino uno as the micronctroller. I then designed and 3D printed all the different pieces. Suprising a hallo box is one of the harderst thing to 3D print without warping. Once that was complete I threw a state machine together and was able to embed a little bit of life into the box. It was fun to feel like I was bringing to life a pixar film on my kitchen counter!
