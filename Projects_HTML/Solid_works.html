Summary

I began my career studying mechanical engineering, and as you'll see, some of my projects incorporate mechanical aspects. I completed two years in the mechanical engineering program and learned SolidWorks along the way. On this page, I'm showcasing a few of my projects, but many others were also designed and printed by me, including the cars and the useless box.

The class itself was both fun and challenging. We were required to complete timed design tests, where we had to design, create corresponding drawings, and implement any necessary motion. The drawings also had to meet the correct tolerances specified in the project. Overall, I'm glad I acquired these skills, even if they aren't widely used in computer engineering.
