Line Follower 2000

Main Concepts

  • State machines
  • Sensor interfacing
  • Arduino Platform

Materials Used

  • Arduino Uno
  • Ultrasonic Sensor HC SR04
  • Reflective Object Sensor QRD1114 (Line detector)
  • 2 DC motors
  • H-Bridge
  • 8 AA batteries
  • Breadboard, 3 LEDs

Summary

This project started out as a mechatronics club competition. However, my team was young and didn't really know what we were doing. We weren't able to complete it in time and failed to enter the competition. Later that summer, I decided not to throw it away and worked on it by myself until I eventually got it working. It was satisfying to have it finally follow the line and perform some of the other functions. It did look a little "drunk" because I spaced the leading line sensors too far apart, but I didn't want to re-solder the entire protoboard. In the end it was fun to build my first robot!
