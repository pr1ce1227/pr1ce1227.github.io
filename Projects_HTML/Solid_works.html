Summary

I began my carreer studying mechanical engineering as you will see some of my projects have mechanical aspects. I completed 2 years in the mechanical engineering program and along that path learned solid works. On this page I'm showing a few of my projects, however many of my other projects were also designed, and printed by myself including the cars and useless box. The class itself was very fun but also very challenging. We were required to do timed design tests where we would have to complete a design, its corresponding drawings and implement any motion if required. The drawings also had to have the correct tollerances to match the project spec. Overall I'm glad I learned this skill even if it isn't widely used in computer engineering.
