Useless Box

Main Concepts

  • 3D Printing
  • Servo Motors
  • State machine

Materials Used

  • Arduino Uno
  • 2 Stepper Motors
  • AA batteries
  • Custom 3d printed parts

Summary

You've probably seen these guys on the internet, as I did many years ago. I decided to take on the challenge of building one myself from scratch, without any tutorials. I used a few servo motors and an Arduino Uno as the microcontroller. I also designed and 3D printed all the different pieces. Surprisingly, a hollow box is one of the trickiest things to 3D print without warping.

Once the printing was complete, I put together a state machine and managed to add a bit of life to the box. It was a lot of fun to feel like I was bringing a Pixar film to life right on my kitchen counter!
