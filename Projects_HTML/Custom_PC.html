Summary

It's hard to go through computer engineering and not want to build your own PC when you're done. This build was mainly for engineering as I am not a big PC gamer. However, I couldn't resist throwing on the cool RGB lighting. It was an interesting project because, despite understanding how CPUs and GPUs work, I didn't know which ones to buy. This led me into a deep rabbit hole of researching different hardware specifications and ensuring compatibility between all components.

I spent over 200 hours researching before finally deciding on this build. The process was fun but also a bit nerve-racking, especially when putting everything together. It was my first time starting with only a BIOS and manually installing an operating system. In the end, I was really happy with the result, and it was quite an upgrade from my dual-core i7 MacBook Air!
