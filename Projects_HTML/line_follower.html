Materials Used

  • Arduino Uno
  • Ultrasonic Sensor HC SR04
  • REFLECTIVE OBJECT SENSOR QRD1114 (Line detector)
  • 2 DC motors
  • H-Bridge
  • 8 AA batteries
  • Breadboard, 3 LEDs

Summary

This project started out as a mechatronics club competition. My team however was young and didn't really know what we were doing. We weren't able to get it done in time and failed to enter into the competition. I decided later that summer that I didn't want to just throw it away so I worked on it by myself until I eventually got it working. It was satisfying to have it finally follow the line and follow some of the other functionality. I know it look a little drunk haha I spaced the leading line sensors to far apart but I didn't want to re-sodder the entire proto board.
